TRADING PRODUCT

Client oriented, technology-enabled and driven by smart quantified strategies
We are structuring our trading products with the vision to show the power & potential of your capital using our strategies as a boat. Our investment process is driven by a tireless pursuit to understand how the markets and trading systems work, using cutting edge technology to validate and execute on timeless and universal trading principles.

Our strategies are specially designed for index futures
The strategies are born out of traditional technical analysis, price action analysis & unique ways of using the same. We trade using strategies which are being tested rigorously over the historical data. Once the strategies show decent numbers, it's tested on the OS data (Out of the sample data). This is the data on the basis of which the strategies were never tested or twisted. This is a systematic process to avoid curve fitting (Strategy adjusted into data). Following the entire process of quantitative analysis, the strategies are paper traded & capital is deployed with religious execution.
